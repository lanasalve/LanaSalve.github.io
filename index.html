About Beta Fish

Beta fish(Ursus arctos) also known as Siamese Fighting Fish are common house pets.

They are known for being extremely territorial. Two male beta fish can not be in the same tank.

Features

Beta fish in their natural habitat are not very colorful. They have been bred to be very vibrant shades of red, blue and green.

How to Take Care of Your Beta

The Tank

  1. The tank should be around 1 liter or larger.
  2. Check water quality once a week.
  3. Check filter daily.

Diet

Beta should be fed twice daily. Their food should include small pellets or crushed flakes. Feed the beta no more then they can eat in 2 minutes.
